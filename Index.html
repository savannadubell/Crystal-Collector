Savanna's Crystal Collector

Instructions for the Game


  1. The computer will give you random number at the start of the game."Number To Find"
  2. There are four crystals below.
    • Yellow
    • Red
    • Blue
    • Green
  3. When you click on a crystal, they will add a specific amount of points to your score.
  4. You will win the game by matching your total score to the random number.
  5. You will lose the game if your total score goes above the random number.
  6. Each crystals value is hidden from you until you click on the gems.
  7. Each time the game starts the game will change each crystals value will change.
